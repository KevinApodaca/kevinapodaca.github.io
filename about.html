About me



I am a Junior computer science student at the University of Texas at El Paso with a focus on Software Development. I am actively looking for internship opportunities to put my skills to the test and to prepare myself for the world after college.


I was born and raised in El Paso, Tx and I took an interest in this field during my third year of high school when I took an elective computer programming class, here I learned the basics of Java and developed a couple of applications. It was also here that I was introduced to the world of web design/development. I learned as much as I could and continued to take more computer classes to increase my skill set.


I am currently working on an iOS application with a small team of developers, that will be used by the Humane Society of El Paso volunteers to keep track of their volunteer hours, sign themselves up for volunteer events, and view the pets that are available for adoption at the shelter.
