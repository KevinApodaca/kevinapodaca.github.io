uBeatCS (A community-driven app to share resources)

An Android app designed to help UTEP Computer Science students find resources to pass their classes, resources that are not given to them in class and that can be of great help, such as previous quizzes, coding tutorials, exam reviews.

  • github.com/KevinApodaca/uBeatCS

Currently planning on implementing in Flutter for cross-platform development and release on App Store

Technologies
